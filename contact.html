FAQ

No sound is playing

Due to browser autoplay policies, audio may not start immediately after opening the page. Try clicking anywhere on the screen or adjusting a volume slider before pressing play.

My settings are not being saved

Focus Mixer uses browser local storage to save your settings. Settings may not persist in private / incognito browsing mode.
